We encourage parents or guardians to guide minors under the age of 18 in using our games and services. If you are a minor under the age of 18, please submit your personal information and use our services with the express consent of your parent or guardian. If you, your parents or other guardians require us to correct or delete your personal information, we will take prompt measures to correct or delete it, unless otherwise provided by laws and administrative regulations.

	At the same time, we attach great importance to the protection of children's personal information, and all minors under the age of 14 will be considered children. Children must obtain the consent of their guardian before using our game services. Children should not create their own personal account without the consent of a parent or guardian. If you are the legal guardian of a child, please pay attention to whether the child under your guardianship uses our services or provides his or her personal information with your authorization and consent. If a child's guardian explicitly refuses us to provide services to the child in the above manner, we will promptly delete the relevant children's personal information we collect, store, and use in accordance with legal provisions, and stop providing services to the child.
